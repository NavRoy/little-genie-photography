How It Works

The Process

01
Inquiry

Reach out through our booking form, WhatsApp, or Instagram. Tell us about your event — date, location, and what you're dreaming of. We respond within 24 hours.

02
Consultation

We meet over a call or in person to understand your vision, style preferences, and expectations. We align on the storyline, mood board, and package that fits perfectly.

03
Photoshoot

On the day, we arrive early, blend into the environment, and capture every genuine emotion with our signature cinematic eye — candid, editorial, and beautifully composed.

04
Editing

Our post-production team hand-edits each image with cinematic colour grading, skin-tone perfection, and our signature warm-toned style — turning raw moments into art.

05
Delivery

A curated sneak peek arrives within 7 days. Your full gallery — high-resolution, watermark-free — is delivered via a private online gallery within 2–3 weeks. Lifetime access, always.
