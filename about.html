FINEST PHOTOGRAPHY STUDIO IN KURNOOL

Looking for a photographer who truly understands emotions and storytelling? At Little Genie Photography, we specialise in wedding photography, pre-wedding shoots, portraits, maternity sessions, and cinematic films — beautifully capturing your most meaningful moments with artistry and heart.

Founded in 2016 in Kurnool, Andhra Pradesh, our studio was born from a deep love for visual storytelling. From the quiet glow of a morning ritual to the exuberant joy of a grand celebration, we focus on genuine emotion, timeless detail, and authentic storytelling.

Our artistic approach blends candid, cinematic, and traditional styles to ensure every smile, tear, and celebration is preserved with elegance — pressed into your most cherished album, forever.

What We Believe

Our Core Values

01
Genuine Emotion

We believe the most powerful photographs are rooted in real, unposed human connection — the stolen glances, the tender touches, the unguarded laughter that can never be staged.

02
Cinematic Vision

Every session is approached like a short film — with intentional lighting, thoughtful composition, and a narrative arc that transforms individual frames into a cohesive visual story.

03
Artisan Quality

From the first consultation to the final album, every step is handled with the precision and care of a master craftsperson — because your memories deserve nothing less than perfection.

04
Client-First Always

Your comfort, vision, and trust are our highest priority. We invest time in understanding your story before we ever raise the camera — because the best photographs come from genuine connection.

05
Technical Excellence

We invest in the finest camera systems, lighting equipment, and post-production tools — because artistic vision must be matched by technical mastery to produce truly stunning results.

06
Legacy & Longevity

We create heirlooms, not just images. Every photograph is edited, printed, and archived to the highest archival standards — so your memories remain vivid and beautiful for generations to come.
